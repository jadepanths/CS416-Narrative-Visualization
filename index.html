Global Climate Change Visualization

Introduction

This visualization explores global temperature changes over time, using data from the Berkeley Earth Surface Temperature Study. The dataset includes global land and ocean temperatures starting from 1750: kaggle.com/berkeleyearth.

Understanding Uncertainty

The shaded area around the temperature line represents the 95% confidence interval. This accounts for measurement uncertainties due to historical changes in data collection methods and equipment.
